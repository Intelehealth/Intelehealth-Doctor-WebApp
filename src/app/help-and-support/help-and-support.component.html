How Intelehealth works?

Telemedicine app makes specialits doctor consultations available to the rural populations coming to primary healthcare. It helps in saving patients from travelling miles for healthcare. Telemedicine app allows Health Officers to collect detailed patient complaints and symtoms and generates a comprehensive clinical case history. The app also allows them to capture details of previous medications, diagnostics, prescriptions and treatment.
